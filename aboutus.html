Cardiothoracic Surgery

Cardiothoracic surgery specializes in surgical treatment of diseases affecting the heart, lungs, esophagus, and chest. It includes procedures like coronary artery bypass, heart valve repair, and lung resections. This field combines advanced techniques to improve patient outcomes and restore vital organ function with precision and care.

  • Treats diseases of the heart, lungs.
  • Includes procedures like heart bypass.
  • Requires specialized surgeons trained in cardiac care.
  • Aims to improve organ function
  • Minimally-invasive
